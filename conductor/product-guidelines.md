# Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose Style and Tone
- **Modern, Friendly, and Warm:** The language should be highly welcoming and conversational, making guests and staff feel at ease while maintaining a modern and clean approach.
- **Clear and Direct:** Ensure that all instructions and prompts are easy to understand. Avoid unnecessary jargon; prioritize clarity and user friendliness.

## 2. Branding and Visual Identity
- **Color Palette:** Clear and warm tones. The primary theme should leverage clean white and warm orange accents.
- **Theme Support:** The application MUST support both Light and Dark modes seamlessly across all platforms.
- **Modern and Clean Approach:** Visual elements should focus on whitespace, clean lines, and intuitive layouts.

## 3. Core UX Principles
- **Frictionless Booking:** The booking process must be as streamlined as possible. Minimize steps, reduce form fields to essentials, and prioritize speed and efficiency from room discovery to checkout.
- **Easy to Use:** Navigation should be intuitive for both first-time guests and busy hotel administrators.
